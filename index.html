Features

Everything to keep
your Mac running fast.

MacCare scans 8 categories of system junk, finds duplicates, manages startup items, and shows exactly where your storage went — with built-in safety so nothing important gets deleted.

🧹
Smart Cleanup

Parallel scanning across 8 junk categories. Risk-classified items with color-coded safety indicators. Only safe items are selected by default.

🗺
Visual Disk Map

Interactive TreeMap visualization shows exactly where your space went. Drill-down navigation, color-coded by file type, with protected paths clearly marked.

🛡
Safety Guard

Hardcoded whitelist of paths that are never touched — Documents, Photos, Music, Mail, SSH keys. Every deletion goes to Trash with full Put-Back support.

📦
Complete Uninstaller

Removes apps and all their hidden remnants — caches, preferences, containers, launch agents, logs. Finds what Finder misses.

⚡
Startup Optimizer

Manage Login Items, LaunchAgents, and system daemons. Toggle items on/off with impact indicators. Keep your Mac booting fast.

📊
Menu Bar Monitor

Always-visible disk space indicator in your menu bar. Quick-scan access and last cleanup stats at a glance.

🎵 For Creative Professionals
Audio Producer Mode

Use Logic Pro, Ableton, or Pro Tools? MacCare automatically detects your DAW and protects all audio-related files — plugins, sample libraries, presets, and caches. No other cleaner does this.

One toggle. Total protection. Clean your Mac without fear of breaking your setup.
